## FAQ: How to Launch on Product Hunt

### When should I launch on Product Hunt?

**12:01 AM Pacific Time** — This maximizes your 24-hour window. Product Hunt resets daily at midnight PT, so launching at 12:01 AM gives you the full day to accumulate upvotes.

Avoid: Mondays (competition is fierce) and weekends (lower traffic).
Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday.

### How many upvotes do I need to win #1?

Based on our 30 wins:

| Ranking | Typical Upvotes |
|:--------|:----------------|
| #1 Daily | 500-1,000+ |
| Top 5 | 300-500 |
| Top 10 | 150-300 |

But upvotes alone don't determine ranking. **Engagement quality matters more** — comments, time spent, conversion rate.

### Do I need a hunter?

**No, you can self-hunt.** Product Hunt changed their algorithm — having a famous hunter no longer guarantees success. What matters:

- Quality of your product page
- Authentic community engagement
- Your maker comment story

### What should my maker comment say?

Your maker comment is your **most important asset**. Structure:

1. **Hook** — Why you built this (personal story)
2. **Problem** — What pain point you're solving
3. **Solution** — How your product helps
4. **Ask** — Invite feedback, not upvotes

❌ Don't say: "Please upvote!"
✅ Do say: "I'd love your honest feedback on X"

### How do I promote without looking spammy?

The golden rule: **Ask for feedback, not votes.**

| ❌ Bad | ✅ Good |
|:-------|:--------|
| "We just launched! Please upvote!" | "We just launched and would love your feedback on X" |
| Mass DMs asking for upvotes | Personal messages to relevant communities |
| Buying upvotes | Building genuine pre-launch audience |

## The 5 Biggest Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

### 1. Launching Without Preparation

**Mistake:** Submitting at random and hoping for the best.
**Fix:** Minimum 4 weeks prep. Build audience first.

### 2. Asking for Upvotes

**Mistake:** "Please upvote my product!"
**Fix:** Ask for feedback. Engagement matters more than raw votes.

### 3. Ignoring Comments

**Mistake:** Not responding to Product Hunt comments.
**Fix:** Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours. This signals engagement to the algorithm.

### 4. Poor Visuals

**Mistake:** Low-quality screenshots, no video.
**Fix:** Invest in a 30-60 second product video. Use high-res screenshots with annotations.

### 5. Wrong Timing

**Mistake:** Launching on Monday or weekend.
**Fix:** Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT.
