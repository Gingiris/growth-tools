I've launched on Product Hunt **30+ times** and won #1 Daily on 30 of them — across AFFiNE and other products. This is the complete, phase-by-phase checklist I use every time.

**TL;DR:**
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT on a Tuesday–Thursday
- Build your seed audience 4–6 weeks before launch (400+ supporters)
- Upvote *velocity* in the first 6 hours matters more than total count
- Ask for feedback, never for upvotes
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours

---

## How the Product Hunt Ranking Algorithm Actually Works

Most makers assume the product with the most total upvotes wins. The reality is more nuanced — **PH ranks products by upvote velocity** in the first 6-8 hours.

The formula is roughly: `upvotes ÷ hours since posting = ranking score`

A product with 50 upvotes in 2 hours (25/hour) outranks one with 80 upvotes in 8 hours (10/hour).

The winning formula combines three forces:
1. **Organic upvote velocity** from genuinely interested users
2. **Community mobilization** through networks you build beforehand
3. **Maker engagement** through thoughtful comments

The expected results by launch tier:

| Result | Upvotes | Traffic | GitHub Star Impact |
|--------|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **#1 Daily** | 500-1,000+ | 20,000-50,000 | 200-500+ stars |
| **Top 5 Daily** | 200-500 | 5,000-20,000 | 50-200 stars |
| **Top 20 Daily** | 50-200 | 1,000-5,000 | 10-50 stars |
| **No traction** | <50 | <500 | Minimal |

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4-6 Weeks Before)

- [ ] **Define your goal** — email signups, GitHub stars, or paying customers? Know what success looks like
- [ ] **Build a seed audience of 400+ supporters** — newsletter subscribers, Twitter followers, beta users, community members you've helped
- [ ] **Engage in the PH community first** — comment on launches, post in Discussions, build relationships before launch day
- [ ] **Find your Hunter** — or self-hunt (both work in 2026). If using a hunter, pick one with 500+ followers who posts in your niche
- [ ] **Prepare your PH assets:**
  - Thumbnail: eye-catching, 240x240px
  - Gallery images: 5-8 high-quality screenshots showing key features
  - Demo GIF or video: 30-60 second loop of core functionality
  - Tagline: under 60 characters, benefit-focused
- [ ] **Write your product description** — Problem → Solution → Proof format
- [ ] **Create a launch-specific landing page** with social proof, features, and email capture CTA
- [ ] **Draft your maker comment** now (see template below)
- [ ] **Line up 3-5 people** from your network to post first-hour comments

---

## Phase 2: Launch Day (12:01 AM PT)

- [ ] **Post at 12:01 AM PT** — This gives you the full 24-hour window
- [ ] **Post your maker comment immediately** (within 2 minutes of going live)
- [ ] **Notify your seed audience** — "We just launched, would love your feedback" (NOT "please upvote")
- [ ] **Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours** — high engagement signals quality to the algorithm
- [ ] **Share on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, relevant Discord/Slack communities** with genuine framing
- [ ] **Post in relevant subreddits** (r/startups, r/SaaS, niche communities) with value, not spam
- [ ] **Monitor your upvote velocity hourly** — if dropping below top-5 pace, mobilize secondary network
- [ ] **Update your PH post** with user testimonials or media mentions throughout the day

## The Maker Comment Template

Your first comment sets the tone. Structure:

1. Personal greeting with name
2. The problem you solved (personal story)
3. 3-4 key features with emojis
4. Promo code or exclusive offer for PH community
5. Engagement question at the end

```plaintext
Hi Product Hunt! 👋

I'm [Name], and I built [Product] because [personal problem/story].

Here's what makes it different:
✅ Feature 1 — [one-line benefit]
✅ Feature 2 — [one-line benefit]
✅ Feature 3 — [one-line benefit]

Use code HUNT20 for 20% off for the PH community.

What's the #1 workflow you'd use this for? 👇
```

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (24-72 Hours After)

- [ ] **Capture the traffic spike** — add a PH-specific banner or limited-time offer on your landing page
- [ ] **Convert PH visitors to email subscribers** — PH traffic is fleeting; email is forever
- [ ] **Thank everyone who commented and upvoted** individually
- [ ] **Document the results** — screenshot PH metrics, traffic data, GitHub star changes
- [ ] **Post a launch recap** in your own channels to extend the momentum
- [ ] **Respond to any remaining unanswered comments** within 48 hours
- [ ] **Write a "lessons learned" post** — good for SEO and community goodwill

## 3 Mistakes That Kill Most PH Launches

### 1. Treating PH as a "post and forget" platform

Makers who win #1 treat PH as a **community**, not a submission form. The launch is the culmination of weeks of community work, not the start of it.

### 2. Asking for upvotes before providing value

PH's community is allergic to shameless promotion. Showing up asking for votes without contributing first earns negative reactions — and PH moderators notice low-engagement launches.

**What to say instead:**

| Avoid | Use instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "We just launched! Please upvote!" | "We just launched and would love your feedback on X" |
| Mass DMs asking for upvotes | Personal messages to relevant communities |

### 3. Ignoring upvote velocity

Products with 300 total upvotes can lose to products with 150 upvotes because of better velocity timing. The **first 6 hours are make-or-break**. Concentrate your supporter outreach in that window.

## FAQ

### How many upvotes do you need to win #1 on Product Hunt?

It depends on the day. A slow Tuesday might require 150-200 upvotes; a busy day can require 500-1,000+. More important than total count is upvote velocity — getting 50-100 upvotes in the first 2 hours signals ranking quality to the algorithm.

### When is the best time to launch on Product Hunt?

Launch at **12:01 AM Pacific Time** to get the full 24-hour window. Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday. Avoid Monday (high competition) and weekends (lower traffic).

### Do I need a hunter?

No. Self-hunting works in 2026. The algorithm no longer heavily weights hunter reputation. What matters more: product quality, maker comment, and authentic community engagement.

### Can you launch on Product Hunt more than once?

Yes. PH allows multiple launches for different products or major version updates. We launched AFFiNE multiple times over 18 months.

### Does Product Hunt still drive meaningful traffic in 2026?

Yes. A #1 daily launch drives 20,000-50,000 visitors and 200-500 GitHub stars in 24 hours. Convert those visitors to email subscribers before the spike ends.
